Biography

Bruno Major is an English song-writer based in Camden, London. Bruno was trained as a classical guitarist since a very young age. He started to do modern electronic jazz production in early 2010 which gained him more audience due to the rise of the soul genre.

He received more attention in 2016 when he set out to release a new song every month of a full year, this project would extend into 2017 and culminate in the arrival of the full-length "A dong for Every moon". Throughout 2019 and early 2020, he issued several singles in advance of his second album, "To Let a Good Thing Die", which arrive in June of that year.
